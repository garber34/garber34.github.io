Keep Drawing

An online team-based social game for 4 people. Players waiting for a game are placed in a waiting room and can engage in text chat to other players. When enough players have joined the waiting room, they are randomly paired in teams of two and placed in a game. One player (the "clue giver") is shown an image and must describe it verbally to their teammate (the "drawer"). The drawer attempts to draw the described image by tracing it with their hands in front of their webcam. The drawn image is shown to the clue giver in realtime and the clue giver can submit the drawing at any time. When submitted, the team scores points based on how similar the drawing is to the original image and the roles reverse. The team with the highest score when the time runs out wins!
